Pokemon

Pokemon are made up creatures. There are 802 Pokemon, I'll name a few: Mewtwo, Pikachu, Mew, Guzzlord, Charmander and loads more.

Pokemon can evolve into better versions of themselves [for example, Pichu evolves into Pikachu, and Pikachu evolves into Raichu] and some can turn into megas. Since there are so many Pokemon there are regions where Pokemon live.

There are seven regions: Kanto, Johto, Heoenn, Sinnoh, Unova, Kalos, and Alola.

the rarest Pokemon in the world are Legendarys and Ultra Beasts[only found in Alola] the starters in Kanto are Charmander, Pikachu, Squirtle and Bulbasaur.
